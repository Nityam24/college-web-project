Welcome To PARTH Institute

Parth Classes began its journey in the year 2004. Started as an institution preparing students to compete in IIT JEE (Indian Institute of Technology Joint Entrance Examination). IIT Ashram has now widened its horizon. Now it offers courses for JEE Main(AIEEE), JEE Advanced(IIT-JEE), NEET(AIPMT), AIIMS, GUJCET and Foundation courses for the students of Secondary School.

The primary objective of the institute is to build a strong knowledge base in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology among students to enable them to face the competitive world.

The strength and success of the institute lies in its highly qualified, dedicated and experienced teaching staff who have been handpicked from premier institutions in the country.
